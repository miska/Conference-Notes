Open Advice
===========
:url:        http://open-advice.org
:conference: FOSDEM 2012
:presenter:  Lydia Pintscher
:presented:  4/2/2012

Book about things that people wished to know before join community. Talk was
about writing the book. They used git to write it, used people to get more
people. No boundaries were set, some people wasn't really sure what to do.
Nobody follows deadlines. Although people liked the idea, they might not
contribute.
